The CASTOR Workshop on Dependable and Secure Systems is a halve day workshop on November 28. 2020, organized by the CASTOR Software Research Centre. The workshop program includes invited talks of industrial and academic speakers focusing on dependable and secure systems and aims at promoting a discussion of industry-academia cooperation in the area of dependable and secure systems.

When: November 28. 2020 (8:55 - 12:30)
Where: Online (Zoom)
